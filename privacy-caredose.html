Privacy Policy — CareDose

Last updated: May 5, 2025

CareDose ("we", "our", or "the app") is a medication reminder and tracking application developed by Sokyra Labs. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

CareDose stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or health data on external servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Medication data — medication names, dosages, schedules, and intake history you enter.
  • Profile data — profile names and colors you create within the app.
  • App settings — notification preferences and display settings.

2. How We Use Data

  • To display your medication schedules and send local reminder notifications
  • To track dose history and calculate adherence statistics
  • To generate PDF and CSV reports that you can share from your device

3. Data Sharing

CareDose does not share any data with third parties. The app has no internet connection, no analytics, and no advertising. All data remains exclusively on your device.

When you choose to export a report (PDF or CSV), the file is created locally on your device and shared only through the standard system share dialog — entirely under your control.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on your device using the standard Android local storage (SharedPreferences). You can delete all app data at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the app from your device settings.

5. Notifications Permission

CareDose requests permission to send local notifications in order to deliver medication reminders. Notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external servers or services.

6. Children's Privacy

CareDose is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently removes all your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
