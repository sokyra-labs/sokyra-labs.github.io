Privacy Policy — Ignis

Last updated: April 16, 2025

Ignis ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered calorie and nutrition tracking application developed by Sokyra Labs. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Ignis stores all nutrition data (meals, diary entries, goals) locally on your device. We do not collect or store your personal health data on our servers.

The following information may be processed:

  • Nutrition data — meals, calorie logs, macros, and health goals you enter. Stored locally only.
  • Food photos — when you use AI photo recognition, the image is sent to Google Gemini API to identify food and estimate nutrition. Photos are not stored on our servers.
  • AI queries — text messages to the AI assistant are sent to Google Gemini API. No conversation history is stored on our side.
  • Advertising data — the app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide core app functionality (calorie tracking, diary, statistics)
  • To identify food and estimate nutrition from photos via Gemini API
  • To generate AI-powered nutrition insights and recommendations
  • To display relevant advertisements via AdMob

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Gemini API — food photos and anonymized queries for AI responses
  • Google AdMob — advertising identifiers for ad delivery

4. Data Storage and Security

All your nutrition data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. You can export and delete your data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

5. Camera Permission

Ignis may request access to your device camera to enable AI food photo recognition. Camera access is used solely for this purpose and only when you explicitly initiate a photo scan.

6. Children's Privacy

Ignis is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings. For any privacy-related requests, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
