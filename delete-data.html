Delete Your Data

Kapva — AI Finance Tracker · Sokyra Labs

All your financial data (accounts, transactions, categories, debts, and goals) is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. You are in full control of your data at all times.

Option 1 — Delete data directly from the app

  1. Open Kapva on your device
  2. Go to Settings → Data
  3. Individual items can be deleted from their respective screens (accounts, transactions, etc.)

Option 2 — Clear all app data (Android)

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Kapva
  3. Tap Storage → Clear Data

This permanently removes all app data from your device.

Option 3 — Uninstall the app

Uninstalling Kapva removes all locally stored data from your device.

Since all data is stored locally, there is no account or server-side data to delete. The steps above are sufficient to remove all your data completely.
